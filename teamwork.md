# Team Contribution and Collaboration

## 1. Team Project Overview

The project report was developed collaboratively as part of a team-based assignment. Responsibilities were clearly distributed among team members, as reflected in the structured allocation (Person 1, Person 2, etc.). This ensured that each member contributed to a specific part of the system design.

---

## 2. My Contribution

My contribution focused on defining the functional and non-functional requirements of the system, as well as justifying the selection of tools and technologies. This included:

- Identifying how the intelligent agent retrieves, processes, and stores academic data  
- Defining system requirements such as reliability, scalability, and modularity  
- Supporting the overall system design and structure  

In addition, I independently implemented the system using Python, which involved:

- Developing the agent controller  
- Implementing the data processing and ranking logic  
- Testing the system and generating outputs  

This demonstrates my ability to translate team-based design into a working system.

---

## 3. Team Meetings

The team conducted two main meetings involving all members to organise the project and distribute tasks. These meetings helped align the team on system design decisions and ensured that each member understood their responsibilities.

---

## 4. Communication and Coordination

In addition to formal meetings, a WhatsApp group was used to support continuous communication. This allowed quick sharing of updates, files, and ideas, ensuring efficient coordination throughout the project.

The use of instant communication tools improved responsiveness and reflects real-world professional practices in virtual team environments.

---

## 5. Challenges and Adaptability

During the project, external challenges were encountered due to geopolitical instability in the Middle East, which affected focus and consistency in work progress.

Despite these challenges, the team maintained communication and adapted to the situation to ensure continued progress. This demonstrates resilience and the ability to work under pressure in a real-world context.

---

## 6. Reflection on Teamwork

Working within the team enhanced my communication and collaboration skills, particularly in a virtual environment. It highlighted the importance of clear task distribution, continuous communication, and adaptability.

The experience also showed the need for stronger critical analysis in team outputs, particularly when moving from descriptive design to deeper evaluation.

Overall, this project strengthened my teamwork, problem-solving, and communication skills, supporting Learning Outcome 4 by demonstrating my ability to work effectively as part of a development team.
