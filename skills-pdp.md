Throughout the Intelligent Agents module, I developed a range of technical and professional skills. These skills were gained through both the team-based design project and the individual system implementation. ## 2. Skills Developed

### Technical Skills

**Python Programming**  
I developed strong Python programming skills by implementing the intelligent agent system. This included writing modular code, designing classes, and implementing data processing logic.

**System Design and Architecture**  
Through the team project, I gained experience in designing system architecture, including modular design and the use of agent-based models such as BDI.

**Data Processing and Analysis**  
I developed skills in handling and processing data, including filtering, removing duplicates, ranking results, and storing structured data in CSV format.

### Professional Skills

**Problem-Solving**  
During implementation, I encountered challenges such as structuring the system and handling data processing. I developed problem-solving skills by breaking down the system into smaller components and resolving issues step-by-step.

**Teamwork and Collaboration**  
Working in a team helped me improve communication and coordination skills. Task distribution and group discussions ensured that the project progressed effectively.

**Time Management**  
Managing both team responsibilities and individual implementation required effective time management to meet deadlines.

**Communication Skills**  
Participating in discussions and documenting the system improved my ability to explain technical concepts clearly.
